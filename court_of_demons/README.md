# Court of Demons

This script is themed around a royal court, featuring a cast of evil politicians and violent wrongdoers. Among the schemers are the Vizier and the Baron, who manipulate the court from the shadows. Alongside them are more direct threats like the Boomdandy and Al-Hadikhia, whose actions bring chaos and destruction.

The setting and characters are designed to evoke the intrigue, danger, and dark politics of a demonic royal court.
